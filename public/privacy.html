Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

NoorQuran is a free Islamic companion app for Quran reading, audio recitation, Salah times, Adhkar, Duas, Tasbih, Qibla direction, reminders, and offline reading support.

Information we collect

NoorQuran does not require account registration and does not ask visitors to sign in. The app is designed to work mainly on your device using local storage.

The app may use the following browser/device permissions only when you choose to enable them:

  • Location: used to calculate prayer times and Qibla direction.
  • Notifications: used for optional prayer or Adhkar reminders.
  • Local storage/cache: used to save settings, bookmarks, notes, offline Quran text, reminder preferences, and app preferences on your device.

How information is used

Location is used only to calculate Islamic utility features such as prayer times and Qibla direction. Notification permission is used only to send optional reminders. Local data is used to keep your personal app preferences and offline reading content available on your device.

Data sharing

NoorQuran does not sell your personal data. NoorQuran does not require a user account. Some features may request public Islamic content or timing data from external content services, such as Quran text, recitation audio, or prayer time APIs, in order to provide the requested feature.

Third-party content/services

The app may load Quran translations, recitation audio, and prayer time information from public Islamic data services. These services may process standard technical request information such as IP address, browser/device information, and request time according to their own policies.

Children

NoorQuran is family-friendly and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Parents or guardians should supervise device permissions such as location and notifications.

Your choices

  • You can deny location permission and use city search instead.
  • You can disable notifications from your browser or device settings.
  • You can clear local/offline data from the app Settings or browser storage settings.
