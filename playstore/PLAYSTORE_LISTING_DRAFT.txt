APP NAME
NoorQuran

SHORT DESCRIPTION (80 characters max)
Quran, Salah, Qibla, Adhkar, Duas, Tasbih and reminders in one app.

FULL DESCRIPTION
NoorQuran is a free Islamic companion app built for daily worship and spiritual organization.

Features include:
• Quran reading with translations
• Quran audio recitations from multiple reciters
• Bookmarks and personal notes
• Offline Quran reading support
• Salah prayer times and monthly timetable
• Qibla direction finder
• Morning and evening Adhkar
• Daily Duas
• Tasbih counter
• Reminder notifications
• Light and dark mode
• Installable mobile app experience

NoorQuran is designed for clean, simple, public access. No account is required.

Important note: Users should verify religious content and timing settings according to their local scholar, masjid, or trusted authority where required.
